Composition 01

2017, audiovisual performance & installation

What does DNA manipulation mean for the human condition? How far do we allow this technology to be developed and applied? Where should lines be drawn?

Those are a few questions we dealt with during the show resolving around the advances made in gene editing techniques. New gene editing techniques such as CRISPR-Cas9 allowing for the manipulation of specific genes.

Together with Jiste Tilman and Rafael Henneberke we have developed a multilayered show that is performed from inside a semi transparent installation which sits in the middle of the audience.

Inside the installation there are 8 musicians performing the music Jiste Tilman has written. Projected on the installation are the visuals me and Rafael Henneberke have created. Together with additional lighting in and outside the installation this resulted in being able to switch between seeing the musicians inside the installation or only its surface with projected visuals.
