#What is Design?

2019, exhibition design

Design is a label that pops up everywhere. But if everything is destined to be designed, design disappears into nothing. Now that design as a discipline has fallen apart, we are taking stock at @droog. That design is everywhere is to be welcomed. Products, environment, services and services have been improved on all fronts. Optimized, they return in an improved version. Society is more sustainable, looks better and is more user-friendly. The task is completed. On to the next challenge for the ambitious designer: the design of ourselves.

The exhibition #What is Design is an interactive project by Mieke Gerritzen based on the manifesto she co-authored with Geert Lovink of the same name. The exhibition is aimed at questioning design.

Niels Schrader and me have transformed the manifesto in an oversaturated typographic world full of questions and statements.
