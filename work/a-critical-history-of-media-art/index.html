Composition 01

2017, audiovisual performance & installation

A Critical History of Media Art in the Netherlands: Platforms, Policies, Technologies is an edited volume offers an in-depth exploration of Dutch media art from 1985 onwards from many different perspectives.

Through early access to the Internet, state subsidies and dedicated institutions and festivals, a vivid counter-cultural environment and a cosmopolitan artistic and intellectual scene the Netherlands hold a unique position in regards to the development of media art.

In collaboration with Jitse Tilman & Rafael Henneberke.
